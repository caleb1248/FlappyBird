In normal mode, click or press spacebar to jump. Every time you pass a pipe, the gravity flips.

In hold4up, you hold to go up or down, depending on if the bird is upside down or not. Every time you pass a pipe, the gravity flips. Don't touch the ground!

In tap4gravity, you tap to flip the birds gravity. When you pass a pipe, the gravity doesn't auto-flip.

Have fun!
